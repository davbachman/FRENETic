# FRENETic

[Open the app](https://davbachman.github.io/FRENETic/)

Created by David Bachman with GPT 5.5. To learn more about David see https://pzacad.pitzer.edu/~dbachman/, and subscribe to his AI podcast *Entropy Bonus* at https://profbachman.substack.com/.

## Brief description
FRENETic is a passive 3D visualization of curvature and torsion along a closed space curve, with a glowing tunnel view, minimap, Frenet-vector overlays, and retro-futuristic invariant gauges.

## Instructions for use
Open the app and watch the camera move along the knot. Use the fullscreen button at the top center to present it as a screensaver-style visualization.
